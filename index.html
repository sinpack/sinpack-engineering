OUR SPECIALTY AND VISION


We're a stable and steadily growing company which main occupation is the research - design - manufacturing of automatic and semi-automatic packaging machinery. We deliver high-tech, individual, efficient, reliable and versatile solutions that answer current and future requests from the market.
Ever since producing our first vertical packaging machine in the early 1980's, we remained exclusively focused on perfecting the vertical packaging machine process through continuous innovation and development.

SPARE PARTS
HOW TO ORDER

The most important information to provide when ordering a spare part is the serial number of the item, which can be found on the nameplate below the main switch.

Use the detailed spare parts catalog and the instructional video supplied with your machine.

Spare parts can be found in a standard search of the parts catalog, which is part of the machine’s manual. Alternatively, you can take a picture of the part and identify it with your finger or an arrow. To order a spare part, simply use the order number of the required part.
